About Me

I am a Grade 12 student from Ethiopia with a strong passion for technology, education, and community leadership. As a member of the Youth Advisory Council , I actively leverage my leadership and digital skills to create meaningful impact in my community. Through the Kelem Ethiopia Equip Project, I have delivered academic training to 20+ students at Kombolcha High School and conducted public speaking sessions for 200+ parents in school on education, social change, addiction, and gender equity. Additionally, I have mentored 40+ students on sexual and reproductive health through the Kefeta Project. By combining my leadership experience with my passion for technology and web development, I aim to drive positive social impact locally and beyond.
